MEET OUR TEAM

Dr. Yiyan, Xue

RAc and RTCMP

Yiyan is an R.Ac and R.TCMP who graduated from the Gansu University of TCM with a Bachelor of Chinese Medicine, an M.D. and a Ph.D. of Chinese Medicine from the Shanghai University of TCM. She holds a certificate of qualifications (2004) and a practicing certificate (2007) for her role as a medical practitioner by the Ministry of Health. She is also recognized by her Senior Title of Physician of TCM by the Ministry of Health since 2014.

She worked for numerous years as a Chinese Medicine Physician in the Long Hua Hospital and as the Chief of the TCM Department in Chingho Medical Hospital, with teaching experience as a lecturer and course coordinator for undergraduate and medical residency students.

Yiyan’s specialties include painless acupuncture for acute and chronic soft tissue injuries, sciatica, frozen shoulder, stiff neck, migraine headaches, herpes zoster, cervical spondylosis, and lumbar disc herniation; Painless anti-aging cosmetic acupuncture; Weight loss acupuncture; Acupuncture dealing with female infertility and irregular menstruation.

Na, Qu

RMT

Na has been practicing Massage Therapy since 2014 when she graduated from the Royal Institute of Science and Management and is in good standing with CMTO. Na’s massage treatments are tailored according to the client’s needs in order to ensure the best results possible. She has experience in the use of therapies as deep tissue, Swedish, trigger point, Chinese Tuina, and hot stone massage. Na enjoys educating patients on ways they can maintain their joints, muscle, and postural health through movement and postural awareness. She is committed to listening to your health goals and upholding the highest quality of care. Na is attending Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine courses and she is always looking for opportunities to advance her knowledge for the needs of her client.

Yali, Jin

RMT

Yali was drawn to massage to provide support to those on their own journey of mental, physical and emotional well-being and is happy to be a member of the Better Health & Wellness team.

She believes that understanding the body and how it works is the foundation needed to give a great effective massage, regardless if the goal is addressing issues or simple relaxation.  Her RMT training provided that foundation.

She is a registered member of CMTO and an active member of the RMTAO.  She is trained and has experience in providing several different types of massage treatments including, Swedish, Deep Tissue, Myofascial release, Trigger point, prenatal and relaxation therapy.  She has a broad knowledge of various modalities including hot stone, reflexology and cupping which promote the flow of qi (life force energy), bringing balance to your body.

Yali take pride in her work recognizing the need for a wholistic approach to meet the unique needs of every client and the importance of educating her clients.

Elena, Guileua

RMT

As a young girl, Elena was known to informally practice massage on family and friends, receiving much positive feedback and outcomes. After a few years of a career in Social Work, Elena finally accepted her calling in Massage Therapy and became a proud graduate of the Sutherland-Chan School and Teaching Clinic in Toronto.

Elena's passion is helping people, so her approach to massage therapy is to help you heal by addressing the problems resulting in pain or discomfort. To do this, she takes the time to listen to your experiences, including a full assessment.

Elena gained experience treating a wide range of conditions and needs, including motor vehicle accidents, sports injuries, post-operative, pre-natal, nervous system disorders (MS), and other chronic conditions.

Elena also enhanced her education at the Thoracic Surgery Unit at the Toronto General Hospital and spinal cord rehab at the Lyndhurst Rehabilitation Institute. Furthermore, she has enriched her background by gaining knowledge and practicing equine massage.
